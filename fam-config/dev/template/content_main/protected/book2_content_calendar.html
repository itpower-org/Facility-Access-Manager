Book something

Apply for something something

  • Step 1: Check the facility and - if relevant - the units you would like to make a reservation for. You can change the facility and / or the units by clicking Change units
  • Step 2: Click on “Request ” and fill out the form for start- and end-time. Alternatively search the calendar and click on the start-time you want.
  • Step 3: Customize start- and end-time to your needs.
  • Step 4: Click on “Check” to get free time slots. If nothing is available, you have to change your requested start- and end-time and / or units.
  • Step 5: Finish your reservation by clicking on an available time slot.
